WebUSB Serial Number

In order to run this test, you need to have a USB device attached to your computer. Most, but not all, devices will work. If you do not see a serial number reported for the device, it means that the device will not work for this test (but also, that the device does not put users at risk for fingerprinting).

  • Disabled: All serial numbers for USB devices should be identical on both test domains (see other site)
  • Default: All serial numbers for USB devices should be different on both both test domains (see other site)
